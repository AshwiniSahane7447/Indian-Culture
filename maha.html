Maharashtrian Food

The cuisine of Maharashtrian are primarily vegetarian as well as Non-vegetarian offers a fabulous variety of mouthwatering dishes. The spice content is quite high in comparison to other Indian cuisines but the food is absolutely scrumptious. Maharashtrian uses ghee and spice for cooking most of the dishes.

There is an enormous variety of sweets in Maharashtrian, which are relished and savored by all. In Maharashtra, the sweet dishes are had before the meal, with the main course and after the meal unlike other desserts. Self service concept is considered rude and thus it does not form part of the etiquettes of Maharashtra.

The cooking style followed in Maharashtra is based on the natural climatic conditions.

    Components in Maharashtrian Food(Regular)
  1. Bajari Bhakar
  2. Jawari Bhakar
  3. Gehu Chhapati
  4. Rice
  5. Sabajee(e.g.Potato,Brinjal and so on)
  6. Onion
  7. Chatani
    Components in Maharashtrian Food(Special)
  1. Puran Poli(Chhapati)
  2. Ghulavani
  3. Bhaje(Pakodda)
  4. Papad
  5. Puri
